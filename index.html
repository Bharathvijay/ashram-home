How to reach here?

From Guwahati, bus, train and chopper services are available to reach Itanagar. Before visiting Arunachal Pradesh, one must procure inner line pass from the Government.

Julie Road to Ashram

In case you don't have any mode of transport to reach ashram from Julie Road, call us and ask for a vehicle service.
